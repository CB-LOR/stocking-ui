Sign up to contribute to the Lowcountry Orphan Relief Stocking Drive which is being coordinated in Cane Bay by Jeannette Meeks.

How it works:

  1. Submit the form below.
  2. We will get the number of stockings you request distributed to you - if picking up from us, we will set them outside our door with your name attached. Otherwise they will be emailed.
  3. Be sure to read the FAQs on the LOR website.
  4. Go shopping for the items on your list.
  5. Drop the items on our porch so that we can deliver to LOR (Or you are welcomed to deliver to LOR yourself).
  6. After dropping the items on the porch, send a text to 412.977.9133 and include your name (or the name that was entered on the signup form) to let us know the stockings have been returned.

Important dates to keep in mind:

  • Last day to get stockings is December 3, 2021.
  • Stockings need to be returned to our residence by December 5, 2021 so that they can be returned to LOR by the deadline.
  • If not able to return to us in time or need to return to LOR yourself, stockings must be returned by December 10, 2021.

For additional information, please refer to the LOR website.
