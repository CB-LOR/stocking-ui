Sign up to contribute to the Lowcountry Orphan Relief Stocking Drive which is being coordinated in Cane Bay by Jeannette Meeks.

How it works - Steps for Cane Bay
  1. Submit the form below.
  2. We will get the number of stockings you request distributed to you - if picking up from us, we will set them outside our door with your name attached. Otherwise they will be emailed.
  3. Be sure to read the FAQs below.
  4. Go shopping for the items on your list.
  5. Drop the unwrapped items on our porch so that we can deliver to LOR (Or you are welcomed to deliver to LOR yourself).
  6. After dropping the items on the porch, send a text to the number we have posted on our door and include your name (or the name that was entered on the signup form) to let us know the stockings have been returned.
 Stocking drive FAQs - Make your gift-giving easy!
  • You do not have to purchase everything on your list. Buy whatever you feel comfortable with!
  • Please do not wrap the presents.
  • Don't get stressed out about the sizes. If you end up buying a size bigger/smaller or if it gets confusing (different stores have different sizing methods), then don't worry about it. We will get the sizes into the right hands!
  • NO toys, puzzles, or games. If you would like to add a "little extra" to your gift, you can bring: stuffed animals, books, school supplies, backpacks, pajamas, socks, water bottles, small soft blankets, accessories, toiletries, diapers/pull ups, wipes & journals.
  • Lowcountry Orphan Relief does NOT have children on site. LOR packs Care Kits for children who are referred to us from schools, local agencies/organizations, churches, and group homes. We pack 2 full weeks of clothing and necessities into each Care Kit, so the children have what they need during their transition. Your gifts will be part of the larger Care Kit given to children throughout the year!
  • For all other questions or concerns, please email Jeannette. Thank You!
 Dates to remember
  • Last day to get stockings is December 3, 2021.
  • Stockings need to be returned to our residence by December 5, 2021 so that they can be returned to LOR by the deadline.
  • If not able to return to us in time or need to return to LOR yourself, stockings must be between December 6, 2021 - December 10, 2021 to LOR's location at 1850 Truxton Avenue, 29404 (the old Navel Base).
